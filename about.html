About Slowdio

Slowdio is Bangladesh's first slow-motion product shot studio. Slowdio uses the power of slow motion to showcase products in a way that is both visually stunning and informative. Slowdio's clients include Square, Pran, MGI, New Zealand Dairy, Akij Bakery, City Group (Teer), and Nestle.
Slowdio believes that slow motion is the perfect way to capture the essence of a product. It allows us to slow down time and see the details that would otherwise be missed. This can be used to highlight the product's features, its quality, or its performance.
If you are looking for a way to make your products stand out, then slow motion is the answer. Contact Slowdio today to learn more about how they can help you create stunning slow-motion product shots.
